My Anime List

A collection of my top anime recommendations

Naruto

A young ninja with a dream to become the strongest leader, the Hokage. Naruto's journey is filled with action, friendship, and self-discovery.

One Piece

Follow Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they search for the legendary treasure, One Piece, in a grand adventure filled with humor, courage, and friendship.

Attack on Titan

Set in a world overrun by giant, man-eating creatures called Titans, humanity struggles to survive behind enormous walls in a gripping tale of war and resilience.

My Hero Academia

In a world where nearly everyone has superpowers called "quirks," a quirkless boy named Izuku Midoriya still dreams of becoming the world's greatest hero.

Demon Slayer

Tanjiro Kamado joins the Demon Slayer Corps to avenge his family and save his sister, Nezuko, in a beautifully animated tale of courage and hope.

Dragon Ball Z

Goku and his friends protect the Earth from powerful villains in this action-packed series that has become a classic in the anime world.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric seek the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies after a disastrous alchemy experiment.

Death Note

Light Yagami gains a notebook that grants him the power to kill anyone by writing their name, setting off a cat-and-mouse game with a brilliant detective.

Tokyo Ghoul

In a world where flesh-eating ghouls exist, Kaneki's life changes forever after he survives a deadly attack and transforms into a half-ghoul.

Hunter x Hunter

Gon Freecss embarks on an adventure to find his father, learning the art of hunting and meeting friends and foes along the way.
